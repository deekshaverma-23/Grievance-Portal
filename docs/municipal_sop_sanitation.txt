Sanitation Department handles:
- Sewage overflows
- Garbage collection
- Public bins overflow
- Mosquito breeding and health hazards

Protocol:
1. Identify hot spots via complaint categories.
2. Immediate containment if health risk is present.
3. Deploy sanitation crews within 4 hours for high risk.
4. Clear routine garbage within scheduled rounds.
Priority:
Open sewage > overflowing bins > minor litter issues
