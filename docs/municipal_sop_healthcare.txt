The Municipal Healthcare Department handles:
- Hospital ICU failures
- Ambulance response delays
- Medicine stock shortages in public clinics
- Public health emergencies

Process:
1. Acknowledge complaint within 1 hour.
2. Dispatch assessment team within 2 hours for critical cases.
3. Provide immediate patient support (ambulance/alternate facility) if needed.
4. Document actions taken and update grievance status.

Escalation:
If unaddressed within 4 hours, escalate to District Health Officer.
