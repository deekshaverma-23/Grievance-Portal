Electricity Services SOP:
- Transformer fires or exposed LIVE wires: immediate blackout and emergency team dispatch within 1 hour.
- City-wide outages: restoration planning within 4–8 hours.
- Voltage fluctuations: inspection within 24 hours.
- Street lighting failures: correction within 48–72 hours.

Department roles:
- Field engineer
- Dispatch team
- Controller of utilities
Escalate unresolved tasks after target SLA to senior engineer.
