Water Supply Department SOP:
- Inspect and isolate bursts within 2 hours.
- Deploy tankers for critical shortages.
- Monitor water quality and contamination reports.
- Inform affected zones about service disruptions.

Timeline expectations:
Critical contamination: immediate action within 4 hours.
Major pipe bursts: containment within 6 hours.
Pressure irregularities: response within 24 hours.

Public advisories must be issued if water supply is unsafe.
