Law & Order SOP:
Handled by Police and Civic enforcement agencies.
Critical issues:
- Violence
- Threat to life
- Large public disorder

Standard Issues:
- Encroachment
- Noise complaints
- Unlawful public drinking

Protocols:
1. Acknowledge within 30 minutes.
2. Deploy patrol unit within 2 hours for critical.
3. Medium issues dispatched within 6 hours.

Escalation to senior officers if unresolved.
